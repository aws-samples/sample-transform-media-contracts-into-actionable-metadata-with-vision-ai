Term: Author Copies
Glossary: publishing_literary_rights
Definition: The number of free author's copies the publisher will provide, and the discounted price at which the author may purchase additional copies for personal use, resale at events, or gifting.
Usage Context: Typical terms provide a dozen or more free copies on publication, with additional copies available at the publisher's cost plus a small markup. Authors should negotiate for sufficient copies to support promotion and speaking events.
Source: Authors Guild Model Trade Book Contract — https://authorsguild.org/resource/model-trade-book-contract/
