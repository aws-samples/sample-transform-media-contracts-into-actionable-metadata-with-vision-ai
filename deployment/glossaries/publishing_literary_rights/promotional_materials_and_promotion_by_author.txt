Term: Promotional Materials and Promotion by Author
Glossary: publishing_literary_rights
Definition: Clauses addressing the publisher's obligation to provide promotional materials, the author's obligation to participate in promotion (interviews, tours, social media, etc.), and the allocation of costs between publisher and author.
Usage Context: Authors Guild recommends that the publisher bear the cost of publisher-requested promotion activities. The author should not be obligated to undertake unreasonable promotion at the author's expense. Travel should be at publisher's reasonable cost, not out of the advance.
Source: Authors Guild Model Trade Book Contract — https://authorsguild.org/resource/model-trade-book-contract/
