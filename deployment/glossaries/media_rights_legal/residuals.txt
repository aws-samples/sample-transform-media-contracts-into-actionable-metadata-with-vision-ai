Term: Residuals
Glossary: media_rights_legal
Definition: Ongoing payments to performers, directors, or writers for reuse or continued exploitation of a production beyond initial exhibition. Often governed by guild CBAs (SAG-AFTRA, DGA, WGA).
Usage Context: Talent agreements: 'Performer shall be entitled to residual payments in accordance with applicable SAG-AFTRA collective bargaining agreements.'
Source: media_rights_legal
