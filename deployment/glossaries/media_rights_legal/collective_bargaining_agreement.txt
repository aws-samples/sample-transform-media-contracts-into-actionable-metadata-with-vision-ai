Term: Collective Bargaining Agreement
Glossary: media_rights_legal
Definition: A contract between a union (SAG-AFTRA, DGA, WGA, IATSE) and producers establishing minimum compensation, working conditions, residual formulas, and other terms for union members.
Usage Context: Talent agreements: 'Residuals payable in accordance with applicable SAG-AFTRA collective bargaining agreements.'
Source: media_rights_legal
