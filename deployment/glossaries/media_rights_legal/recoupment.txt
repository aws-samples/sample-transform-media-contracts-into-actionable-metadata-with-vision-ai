Term: Recoupment
Glossary: media_rights_legal
Definition: The process by which a party recovers its investment or advance payments from revenue before the other party begins receiving their share. The recoupment waterfall determines payment priority.
Usage Context: Financial terms: 'The Minimum Guarantee shall be recoupable against revenue share payments.' Until recouped, the licensor receives no additional revenue share.
