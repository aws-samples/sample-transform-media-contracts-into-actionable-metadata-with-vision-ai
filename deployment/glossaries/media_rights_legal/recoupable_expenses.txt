Term: Recoupable Expenses
Glossary: media_rights_legal
Definition: Costs a distributor may deduct from revenue before remitting to the content owner. Common categories: marketing, dubbing/subtitling, technical delivery, insurance, taxes. Watch for uncapped categories.
Usage Context: Distribution agreements: 'Distributor may recoup the following expenses from Gross Receipts after deduction of the Distribution Fee.'
Source: media_rights_legal
