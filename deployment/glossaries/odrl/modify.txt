Term: Modify
Glossary: odrl
Definition: To change existing content of the Asset. A new asset is not created by this action.
Usage Context: This action will modify an asset which is typically updated from time to time without creating a new asset. If the result from modifying the asset should be a new asset the actions derive or extract should be used. (Note that the notion of whether or not the change is significant enough to qualify as a new asset is subjective).
