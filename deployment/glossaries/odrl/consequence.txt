Term: Consequence
Glossary: odrl
Definition: Relates a Duty to another Duty, the latter being a consequence of not fulfilling the former.
Usage Context: The consequence property is utilised to express the repercussions of not fulfilling an agreed Policy obligation or duty for a Permission. If either of these fails to be fulfilled, then this will result in the consequence Duty also becoming a new requirement, meaning that the original obligation or duty, as well as the consequence Duty must all be fulfilled
