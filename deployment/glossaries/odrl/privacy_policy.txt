Term: Privacy Policy
Glossary: odrl
Definition: A Policy that expresses a Rule over an Asset containing personal information.
Usage Context: A Privacy Policy  MUST contain a target Asset, a Party with Assigner  is, a Party with Assignee function, and at least one of a Permission or Prohibition rule that MUST include a Duty. The target Asset SHOULD contain or relate to personal information about the Assignee. The Duty MUST describe obligations on the Assigner about managing the Asset. The Assignee is being granted the terms of the Privacy policy from the Assigner.
