Term: Datetime
Glossary: odrl
Definition: The date (and optional time and timezone) of exercising the action of the Rule. Right operand value MUST be an xsd:date or xsd:dateTime as defined by [[xmlschema11-2]].
Usage Context: The use of Timezone information is strongly recommended. The Rule may be exercised before (with operator lt/lteq) or after (with operator gt/gteq) the date(time) defined by the Right operand. <br />Example: <code>dateTime gteq 2017-12-31T06:00Z</code> means the Rule can only be exercised after (and including) 6:00AM on the 31st Decemeber 2017 UTC time.
