Term: GUID (Podcast Episode)
Glossary: podcast_digital_audio
Definition: A globally unique identifier assigned to each episode in a podcast's RSS feed, designed to be persistent through changes to hosting environment, titles, publication dates, and URLs. IAB recommends using the GUID — not the episode URL, title, or publication date — to identify new episodes for automatic download.
Usage Context: Essential for correct episode identification across hosting migrations and metadata changes. Using URL, title, or date as the identifier can cause duplicate downloads or missed episodes when those fields change.
Source: IAB Podcast Measurement Technical Guidelines v2.1 — https://iabtechlab.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/PodcastMeasurement_v2.1.pdf
