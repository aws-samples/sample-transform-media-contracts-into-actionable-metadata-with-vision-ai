Term: Interference
Glossary: broadcast_fcc
Definition: Unwanted electrical signals or noise causing degradation of reception on a communications circuit. FCC rules allocate spectrum and set technical standards to minimize interference between licensed services.
Usage Context: Interference complaints are a common FCC enforcement matter. Broadcast license coordination and protection zones are designed to prevent harmful interference.
Source: FCC Telecommunications Glossary — https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-247863A2.pdf
