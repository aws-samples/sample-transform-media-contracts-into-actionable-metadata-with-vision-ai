Term: Personal Communications Services (PCS)
Glossary: broadcast_fcc
Definition: Wireless voice and/or data communications systems, typically incorporating digital technology. PCS licenses are most often used to provide services similar to advanced cellular mobile or paging services. Spectrum is divided into three major categories: broadband, narrowband, and unlicensed.
Usage Context: Licensed by the FCC for specific frequency bands. Successor to first-generation cellular service. Basis for most modern mobile network operations.
Source: FCC Telecommunications Glossary — https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-247863A2.pdf
