Term: Operator Service Provider (OSP)
Glossary: broadcast_fcc
Definition: A common carrier that provides services from public phones, including payphones and those in hotels, motels, airports, and similar public locations.
Usage Context: Subject to FCC consumer protection rules including rate disclosure requirements. Relevant to operator-assisted call services.
Source: FCC Telecommunications Glossary — https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-247863A2.pdf
