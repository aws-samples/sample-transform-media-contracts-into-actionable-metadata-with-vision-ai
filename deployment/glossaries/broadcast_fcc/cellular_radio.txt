Term: Cellular Radio
Glossary: broadcast_fcc
Definition: A mobile telecommunications system in which a geographic area (city) is broken into 'cells,' each handled by one transceiver (receiver/transmitter). Cellular technology was the predecessor to PCS and modern wireless broadband.
Usage Context: Foundation of mobile carrier spectrum licensing under FCC jurisdiction. Relevant to distribution considerations for mobile-delivered content.
Source: FCC Telecommunications Glossary — https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-247863A2.pdf
