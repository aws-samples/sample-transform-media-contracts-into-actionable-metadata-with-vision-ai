Term: Cramming
Glossary: broadcast_fcc
Definition: A practice in which customers are billed for enhanced telecommunications features such as voice mail, caller-ID, and call-waiting that they have not ordered. Subject to FCC consumer protection enforcement.
Usage Context: Consumer protection issue prompting FCC rules requiring clear billing and authorization of add-on services. Enforcement actions against carriers for cramming are common.
Source: FCC Telecommunications Glossary — https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-247863A2.pdf
