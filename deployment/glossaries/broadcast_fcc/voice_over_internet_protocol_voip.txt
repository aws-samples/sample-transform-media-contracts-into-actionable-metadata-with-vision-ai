Term: Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP)
Glossary: broadcast_fcc
Definition: A technology that allows users to make telephone calls using a broadband internet connection instead of a regular (analog) phone line. VoIP services are subject to specific FCC rules regarding 911 service, CALEA compliance, and other obligations.
Usage Context: Interconnected VoIP providers are subject to a subset of common carrier regulations. Non-interconnected VoIP services have fewer regulatory obligations.
Source: FCC Telecommunications Glossary — https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-247863A2.pdf
