Term: Slamming
Glossary: broadcast_fcc
Definition: The unauthorized switching of a customer's long distance service from one long distance company to another without the customer's permission. Such unauthorized switching violates FCC rules.
Usage Context: Subject to FCC enforcement action and civil penalties. Consumer protection framework extends to other unauthorized service changes.
Source: FCC Telecommunications Glossary — https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DOC-247863A2.pdf
