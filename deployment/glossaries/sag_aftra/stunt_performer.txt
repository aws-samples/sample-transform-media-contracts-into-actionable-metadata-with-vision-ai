Term: Stunt Performer
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: A performer who executes physical stunts on camera, as distinguished from stunt coordinators. Covered under principal performer provisions of the SAG-AFTRA TV/Theatrical MBA.
Usage Context: Stunt performers receive general performer wage increases plus stunt-specific adjustments. Subject to heightened safety protocols and approval requirements for hazardous work.
Source: SAG-AFTRA Stunt & Safety — https://www.sagaftra.org/contracts-industry-resources/stunt-safety
