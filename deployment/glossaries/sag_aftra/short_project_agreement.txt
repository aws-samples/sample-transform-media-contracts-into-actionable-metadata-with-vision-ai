Term: Short Project Agreement
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: A SAG-AFTRA low-budget agreement covering short films with a budget under $50,000 and running no more than 40 minutes, permitting film festival screenings and free-to-consumer new media platform release.
Usage Context: Allows independent producers to hire SAG-AFTRA talent on short films at reduced rates compared to theatrical scale. Requires signed Performer Contracts and Exhibit G forms for each performer workday.
Source: SAGindie FAQ — https://www.sagindie.org/resources/faq/
