Term: SAG-AFTRA Health Plan
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: The health benefits plan administered by SAG-AFTRA Plans for eligible members, funded by employer contributions under collective bargaining agreements. Eligibility is earned based on covered earnings thresholds.
Usage Context: Receives employer P&H contributions remitted via the Contributions Manager. Members must meet minimum earnings thresholds in a reporting period to qualify for health coverage.
Source: SAG-AFTRA Plans — https://www.sagaftraplans.org
