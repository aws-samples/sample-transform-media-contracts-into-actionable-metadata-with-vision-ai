Term: Production Number
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: A unique identifying number assigned by SAG-AFTRA to a television show or theatrical motion picture for tracking purposes.
Usage Context: Used to track residual payments, performer engagements, and signatory compliance across all episodes and runs of a given production. Appears on all SAG-AFTRA residual statements and deal memo filings.
Source: SAG-AFTRA Residuals Glossary — https://www.sagaftra.org/membership-benefits/residuals
