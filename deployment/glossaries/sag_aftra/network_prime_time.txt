Term: Network Prime Time
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: The evening television viewing hours of 8:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m. (7:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m. on Sundays) on the major U.S. broadcast networks: NBC, CBS, ABC, and FOX (FBC).
Usage Context: Residual rates and reuse fees vary based on whether a broadcast occurred during network prime time versus other dayparts. Used to determine applicable residual schedules and rate tiers.
Source: SAG-AFTRA Residuals Glossary — https://www.sagaftra.org/membership-benefits/residuals
