Term: Independently Created Digital Replica
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: A digital replica created without the performer's participation (often using existing materials) and used in a motion picture to portray the performer in scenes they did not actually shoot.
Usage Context: Performers are generally entitled to compensation (including Pension & Health contributions) for creation and use of their independently created replicas, and for use in additional projects or other media. Residuals apply for uses that would normally generate residuals. Limited exception exists for uses protected by the First Amendment (comment, criticism, scholarship, satire, parody, docudrama, or historical/biographical work).
Source: SAG-AFTRA Regulating AI TV/Theatrical 2023 — https://www.sagaftra.org/sites/default/files/sa_documents/AI%20TVTH.pdf
