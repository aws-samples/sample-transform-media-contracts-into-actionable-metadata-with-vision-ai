Term: Stunt Coordinator
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: A performer who designs, supervises, and coordinates stunt work on a production. Stunt coordinators who do not perform on-camera stunts are eligible for full SAG-AFTRA coverage as of the 2023 TV/Theatrical MBA.
Usage Context: Stunt coordinators are covered by specific provisions of the MBA with their own scale rates and residual entitlements. Earlier MBA cycles secured scale increases of 5% for stunt coordinators on flat deal contracts in television.
Source: SAG-AFTRA Stunt & Safety — https://www.sagaftra.org/contracts-industry-resources/stunt-safety
