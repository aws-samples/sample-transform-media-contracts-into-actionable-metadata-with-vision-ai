Term: Pension Credit
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: A unit which measures the amount of time a performer has worked in the motion picture industry in employment covered by a collective bargaining agreement.
Usage Context: Used to determine a member's eligibility for pension benefits, vesting, and benefit amount. Distinct from Earnings Credit.
Source: SAG-AFTRA Plans Glossary — https://www.sagaftraplans.org/sag-pension/spd/glossary-terms
