Term: Digital Alteration
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: The practice of changing an actor's performance as captured in photography or soundtrack previously recorded by the performer.
Usage Context: Under the 2023 TV/Theatrical Contracts, the performer's explicit consent is required to digitally alter a performance, unless the alteration is substantially as scripted, performed, and/or recorded (i.e., traditional post-production edits and fixes do not require separate consent).
