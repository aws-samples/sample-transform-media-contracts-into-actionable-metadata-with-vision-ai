Term: Signatory
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: An entity that has signed a SAG-AFTRA collective bargaining agreement (such as the Commercials Contract, TV/Theatrical Contract, or one of the Low Budget Agreements) and has agreed to comply with its terms and conditions.
Usage Context: Hiring SAG-AFTRA members generally requires the production entity to first become a signatory. Each signatory receives a seniority number. Signatory status is required for lawful engagement of union talent on covered productions.
Source: SAGindie FAQ — https://www.sagindie.org/resources/faq/
