Term: Foreign Royalties
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: Royalty payments collected by foreign collective management organizations on behalf of performers for uses of their work outside the United States, Canada, and U.S. territories, then distributed by SAG-AFTRA to its members.
Usage Context: Distinct from Foreign Market residuals under U.S. collective bargaining agreements. Members must register with SAG-AFTRA's Foreign Royalties program to receive their share.
Source: SAG-AFTRA Foreign Royalties — https://www.sagaftra.org/membership-benefits/foreign-royalties
