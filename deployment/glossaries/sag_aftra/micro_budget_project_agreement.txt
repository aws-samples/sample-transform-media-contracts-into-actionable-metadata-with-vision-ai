Term: Micro-Budget Project Agreement
Glossary: sag_aftra
Definition: A SAG-AFTRA low-budget agreement covering projects with a total budget under $20,000, allowing independent producers to hire SAG-AFTRA talent on ultra-low-budget productions at substantially reduced scale rates.
Usage Context: Designed for first-time filmmakers and shoestring-budget productions. Still requires signatory status, completed Performer Contracts, P&H contributions, and other core SAG-AFTRA compliance requirements.
Source: SAGindie FAQ — https://www.sagindie.org/resources/faq/
