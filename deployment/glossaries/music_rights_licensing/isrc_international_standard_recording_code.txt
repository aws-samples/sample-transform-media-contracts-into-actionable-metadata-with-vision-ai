Term: ISRC (International Standard Recording Code)
Glossary: music_rights_licensing
Definition: A unique 12-character alphanumeric identifier for sound recordings and music video recordings, standardized under ISO 3901. ISRC enables recordings to be uniquely identified for royalty tracking and rights management across jurisdictions.
Usage Context: Required in most digital distribution and licensing workflows. Appears on master license agreements and sync license agreements to specifically identify the master recording being licensed.
Source: NMPA — https://www.nmpa.org
