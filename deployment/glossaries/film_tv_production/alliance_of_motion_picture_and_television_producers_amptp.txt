Term: Alliance of Motion Picture and Television Producers (AMPTP)
Glossary: film_tv_production
Definition: The trade association that represents major motion picture and television studios, networks, and streaming services in collective bargaining negotiations with Hollywood unions including the WGA, DGA, SAG-AFTRA, and IATSE.
Usage Context: The AMPTP negotiates the WGA MBA, the DGA Basic Agreement, and the SAG-AFTRA TV/Theatrical MBA, typically on roughly parallel three-year cycles. Member companies automatically become signatory to resulting agreements.
Source: AMPTP — https://www.amptp.org
