Term: Contracts Department (WGA)
Glossary: film_tv_production
Definition: The WGA department responsible for contract enforcement, including review of writer deal memos and long-form agreements, investigation of MBA violations, and initiation of grievances against non-compliant signatory companies.
Usage Context: Writers experiencing contract disputes or MBA violations contact the Contracts Department (WGAW: 323-782-4501) for guidance and enforcement action.
Source: WGA Contracts — https://www.wga.org/contracts
