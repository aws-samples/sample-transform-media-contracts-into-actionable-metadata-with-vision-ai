Term: Script Registration
Glossary: film_tv_production
Definition: A service run by both WGA West and WGA East to establish the date of creation of literary property. Registration creates a dated record that can be used as evidence of authorship in disputes.
Usage Context: Commonly used by screenwriters to document authorship before submission of material to production companies, studios, or representatives. Does not confer copyright, but supplements copyright registration with the U.S. Copyright Office.
Source: Writers Guild of America — https://www.wga.org
