Term: cloud-based rendering
Glossary: vfx
Definition: Essentially rendering off site. By “cloud-based” it is meant that the render may occur on many machines spread out across the planet or on specific machines at a distant facility. With cloud rendering one can have infinite number of machines working on a render, making them fast and detailed.
