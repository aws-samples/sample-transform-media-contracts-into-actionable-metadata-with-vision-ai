Term: Lineup (lineup sheet)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: Essentially a road map on how to put a specific shot together usually created by the production side VFX editor and sent to the VFX facility doing the work. This sheet has the specifics on frame rate, warps, layers/elements of the comp, etc.
Source: ves
