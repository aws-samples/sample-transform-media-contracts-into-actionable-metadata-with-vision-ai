Term: multiple-pass rendering
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A technique in which a 3D object or scene is rendered in a series of separate images, each with different lighting or rendering characteristics. Typical passes might include color, shadow, reflection, key light, fill light, backlight. See also multiple-pass photography.
