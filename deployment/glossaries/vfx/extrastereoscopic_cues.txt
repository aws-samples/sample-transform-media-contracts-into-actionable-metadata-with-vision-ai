Term: extrastereoscopic cues
Glossary: vfx
Definition: (stereo) Those depth cues appreciated by a person using only one eye. Also called monocular cues. They include interposition, geometric perspective, motion parallax, aerial perspective, relative size, shading, and textural gradient.
