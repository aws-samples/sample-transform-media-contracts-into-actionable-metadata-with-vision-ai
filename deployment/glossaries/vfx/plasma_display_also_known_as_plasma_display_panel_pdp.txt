Term: Plasma Display, also known as Plasma display panel (PDP)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: is a type of flat panel display that uses small cells containing plasma; ionized gas that responds to electric fields. Until about 2007, plasma displays were commonly used in larger televisions (30 inches [76 cm] and larger). Since then, they have lost nearly all market share due to competition from low-cost LCDs and more expensive but high-contrast OLED flat-panel displays.
