Term: high-dynamic-range imaging (HDRI)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A technique for capturing the extended tonal range in a scene by shooting multiple pictures at different exposures and combining them into a single image file that can express a greater dynamic range than can be captured with current imaging technology.
Source: ves
