Term: Bayer mosaic pattern sensor
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A sensor filter pattern that is 50% green, 25% red, and 25% blue, hence is also called RGBG, GRGB, or RGGB. It is named after its inventor, Bryce E. Bayer of Eastman Kodak.
