Term: Panavision
Glossary: vfx
Definition: (1) A manufacturer of motion picture lenses and cameras. (2) The trade name for a specific widescreen process and lenses developed by the Panavision company. It is an anamorphic format that produces an image with a 2.35:1 aspect ratio. See also anamorphic format, Cinemascope.
