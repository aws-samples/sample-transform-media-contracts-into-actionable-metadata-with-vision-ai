Term: Three-perf
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A technique used to maximize the use of raw film stock in 1.85 formats so that almost no film is wasted. Most cameras use a four-perf pulldown that creates a lot of unused film between the captured images, whereas a three-perf pulldown positions the captured images closer together.
