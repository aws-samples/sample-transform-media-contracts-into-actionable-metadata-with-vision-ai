Term: visual effects (VFX)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A broad term that refers to just about anything that cannot be captured using standard photographic techniques. Visual effects can be accomplished in-camera or via a number of different optical or digital post-production processes. Visual effects are a subcategory of special effects.
