Term: 32-bit image
Glossary: vfx
Definition: In the visual effects world, this term typically refers to any image containing 32 bits of color information per channel. At this bit depth the channels are usually stored with a floating point data representation; hence, a 32 bit-per-channel image is thus also referred to as a float image.
