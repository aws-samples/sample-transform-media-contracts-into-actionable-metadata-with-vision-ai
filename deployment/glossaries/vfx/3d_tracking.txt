Term: 3D tracking
Glossary: vfx
Definition: Unlike 2D tracking, 3D tracking is intended to recreate the full 3D movement of the camera that photographed a particular scene (or, less commonly, the full 3D movement of an object in the scene). See also matchmove.
Source: ves
