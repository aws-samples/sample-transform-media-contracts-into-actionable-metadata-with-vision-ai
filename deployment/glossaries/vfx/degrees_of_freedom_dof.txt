Term: Degrees of freedom (DoF)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: The number of dimensions an object or headset can move or be tracked in 3D space. A 3 DoF system can track position or orientation, but not both simultaneously, while a 6 DoF system can track position and orientation simultaneously.
