Term: SECAM
Glossary: vfx
Definition: Officially this is an acronym for séquentiel couleur à mémoire, but most English speakers use the translation sequential color and memory. SECAM is a standard for color television used in France and a few African and Eastern European nations. It carries 625 lines of resolution, played back at a rate of 25 frames per second.
