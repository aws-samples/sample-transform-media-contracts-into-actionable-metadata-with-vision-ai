Term: 4k resolution
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A general term referring to any digital image containing an × resolution of approximately 4096 pixels. The actual dimensions of a 2k image depend on the aspect ratio of the imagery. A common 4k resolution used in visual effects when working with full-aperture framing is 4096 × 3112.
