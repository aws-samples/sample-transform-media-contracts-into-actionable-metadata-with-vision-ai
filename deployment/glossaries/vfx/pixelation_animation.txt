Term: pixelation animation
Glossary: vfx
Definition: Stop-motion technique in which live actors are used as a frame-by-frame subject in an animated film by repeatedly posing while one or more frames are taken and changing the pose slightly before the next frame or frames. The actor becomes a kind of living stop-motion puppet.
