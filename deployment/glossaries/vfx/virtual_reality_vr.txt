Term: Virtual reality (VR)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A simulated experience that can be similar to or completely different from the real world. Applications of virtual reality can include entertainment (e.g., gaming) and educational purposes (e.g., medical or military training). Other, distinct types of VR style technology include augmented reality and mixed reality.
