Term: depth range
Glossary: vfx
Definition: (stereo) A term that applies to stereoscopic images created with cameras. The limits are defined as the range of distances in camera space from the background point, producing maximum acceptable positive parallax, to the foreground point, producing maximum acceptable negative parallax. See also parallax budget.
