Term: 2k resolution
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A general term referring to any digital image containing an × resolution of approximately 2048 pixels. The actual dimensions of a 2k image depend on the aspect ratio of the imagery. A common 2k resolution used in visual effects when working with full aperture framing is 2048 × 1556.
