Term: particle system
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A 3D computer graphics technique that is used to create a large number of objects that obey well-defined behavioral rules. Useful not only for controlling multitudes of discrete objects such as asteroids or flocks of birds but also as a tool for creating natural phenomena such as fire or smoke.
