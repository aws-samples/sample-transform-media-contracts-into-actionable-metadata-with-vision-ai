Term: “T”
Glossary: vfx
Definition: (stereo) In stereoscopy, “T” is used to denote the distance between the eyes, called the interpupillary or interocular distance; “T” is used to denote the distance between stereoscopic camera heads’ lens axes and is called the interaxial.
