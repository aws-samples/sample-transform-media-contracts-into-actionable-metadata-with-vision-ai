Term: vectorscope
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A device used to view the chrominance portion of a video signal. Radial distance from the center of the display represents saturation (chrominance amplitude), and the counterclockwise or clockwise angular distance represents the hue (chrominance phase). See also waveform monitor.
