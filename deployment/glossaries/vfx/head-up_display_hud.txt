Term: Head-up display (HUD)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A transparent overlay which presents data about the virtual world to a viewer without their having to look away from their current perspective. Used in conjunction with virtual camera to approximate the display of a real-world camera during virtual scouting, techvis, etc.
Source: vp
