Term: High dynamic range imaging (HDRI)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: An omnidirectional set of images shot in a bracketed wide range of exposures that captures the real-world illumination values of an environment. An HDRI is often used as an Image Based Light (IBL) to light virtual assets and environments.
