Term: rotoscope
Glossary: vfx
Definition: Originally the name of a device patented in 1917 by Max Fleischer to aid in cel animation. Now used as a general term for the process of creating imagery or mattes on a frame-by-frame basis by hand. Also referred to as a roto.
