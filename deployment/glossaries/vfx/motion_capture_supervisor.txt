Term: Motion capture supervisor
Glossary: vfx
Definition: The technical supervisor leading a motion capture team responsible for shoots, technology decisions, and is the on-set point-of-contact. Ensures the capture and delivery of the highest quality across all motion capture data and processing to on-set visualization and VFX teams.
