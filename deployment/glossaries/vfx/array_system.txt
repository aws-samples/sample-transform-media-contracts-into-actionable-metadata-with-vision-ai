Term: Array System
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A system where several cameras, with overlapping fields of view, are mounted on a vehicle and used to capture a moving environment. The images are stitched together into one seamless image in order to be able to pan and/or tilt around in that image. Usually used for driving BG plates, train BG plates, etc.
