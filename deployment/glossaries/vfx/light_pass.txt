Term: light pass
Glossary: vfx
Definition: (1) In multiple-pass photography, the pass of individual lights striking the subject, such as the key or fill, for later use in compositing. (2) In multiple-pass rendering, the CG element that represents the effects of a particular light striking the object. See also beauty pass, matte pass, reflection pass, shadow pass.
