Term: Zulu set
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A physical set or prop designed for actors to interact with and stand in as a proxy for a virtual asset. Sometimes built at different scales to accommodate for major size differences in actor to character. This term was coined and still in use by the Avatar team; also referred to as a proxy set.
