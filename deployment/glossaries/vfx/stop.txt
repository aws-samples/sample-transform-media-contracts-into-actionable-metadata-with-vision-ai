Term: stop
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A way of measuring exposure that traces back to the different f-stop settings available on any given lens. The f-stops on a lens are calibrated so that each successive stop will give twice the exposure. Thus, “increase the brightness by one stop” means to double the brightness; “decrease by two stops” would result in one-fourth the original brightness.
