Term: film speed
Glossary: vfx
Definition: A very context-dependent term that may refer to (1) the rate at which film is moving through a camera or a projector (24 frames per second in normal feature-film work) or (2) the light sensitivity of the film itself. Slow-speed film is less light sensitive; high-speed film is more sensitive.
