Term: Ramsdell rig
Glossary: vfx
Definition: (stereo) First designed by Floyd Ramsdell. A rig that allows two cameras to be arranged in a way that provides the means for taking stereoscopic moving images, by use of a beamsplitter, to create an interocular separation that is the same distance apart as typical human eyes. See beamsplitter.
