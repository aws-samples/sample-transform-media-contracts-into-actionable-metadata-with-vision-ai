Term: LIDAR (light imaging detection and ranging )
Glossary: vfx
Definition: (LiDAR and LADAR) A surveying method that measures distance to a target by illuminating the target with laser light and measuring the reflected light with a sensor. Differences in laser return times and wavelengths can then be used to make digital 3-D representations of the target.
