Term: Light Box
Glossary: vfx
Definition: consists of walls of LED lights surrounding an acting space. The LED lights, driven in real time by images pre-rendered from the perspective of the actor, provide correct lighting for the live-action subject and allows the actor to see the CG images of the elements to be added later.
Source: ves
