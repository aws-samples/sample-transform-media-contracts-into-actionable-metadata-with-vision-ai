Term: CBB
Glossary: vfx
Definition: Abbreviation for could be better. When a shot has a few minor technical or aesthetic adjustments to be made, but the delivery date is close at hand, this term is used to final a shot with the caveat that it will be improved at a later date if time and budget permit.
