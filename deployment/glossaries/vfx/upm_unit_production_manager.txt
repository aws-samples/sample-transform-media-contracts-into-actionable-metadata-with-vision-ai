Term: UPM (unit production manager)
Glossary: vfx
Definition: The DGA-approved title for the top below-the-line staff position, responsible for managing the production and regulating the costs of delivering the expected film or television show on budget at the end of principal photography. Non-DGA productions might call them a production manager or production supervisor. They work closely with the line producer. Sometimes the line producer is the UPM.
