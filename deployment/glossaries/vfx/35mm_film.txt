Term: 35mm film
Glossary: vfx
Definition: The most common film format used in professional moviemaking. Each frame contains a gauge of 35mm and four perforations (thus its nickname four-perf) and there are 16 frames per foot. The sound information usually runs along the left side of the film between the perforations and the image.
